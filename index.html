Common Questions

FAQ

Is this just a generic report with my logo on it?

No. Your audit is based on the specific workflow and operational data you provide in the intake questionnaire. Two businesses in the same industry with different team structures, tools, and bottlenecks will get substantially different reports. The framework is consistent — the output is specific to you.

How long does it actually take?

Starter and Enterprise audits are delivered within 5 business days of completing your intake questionnaire. Business audits within 7 business days. If you need it faster, contact us before purchasing and we'll tell you honestly whether we can accommodate it.

What if I don't know much about AI?

Good. This audit is designed for business owners and operators who aren't technical. Every recommendation is written in plain English, every tool is explained in terms of what it does for your business — not how it works under the hood — and every action item is specific enough to hand to a team member to implement.

I've already got some AI tools. Is this still useful?

Almost always yes. Most businesses we work with have started using one or two tools — usually ChatGPT and maybe one automation platform. The audit will assess whether you're using them to their full potential, identify gaps in what you're missing, and help you build a coherent strategy around what you already have rather than starting from scratch.

Will you recommend expensive tools I can't afford?

We flag the cost of every tool recommendation. The audit includes both free/low-cost and premium options. The 90-day plan prioritises quick wins that don't require significant spend. If you're on a tight budget, tell us in the intake questionnaire and we'll weight recommendations accordingly.

Is my business information kept confidential?

Yes. Your intake data is used solely to produce your audit report and is not shared with third parties. We don't sell data. We don't use your information to train AI models. If you have specific data handling requirements (healthcare, legal, financial services), note them in the questionnaire and we'll flag any relevant considerations in the report.

What if I'm not happy with the report?

If the report doesn't give you at least 3 clear, actionable AI opportunities you didn't already know about, we'll refund your payment in full — no hoops, no arguing. Just email us within 14 days of receiving the report. We stand behind the work.

We're a franchise / have multiple locations. Which tier is right?

Enterprise is built for that. Multi-location businesses have more complex operations, more touchpoints where AI can create leverage, and usually more compliance considerations. Enterprise audits can accommodate stakeholder input from multiple sites and will map AI opportunities across your whole operation, not just one location.
